At A Glance

Texas Christian University

I started studying at TCU in the fall of 2020. I plan to graduate by 2024. At TCU, I plan to serve as a representative of the minorities in the student body. I plan to bring about inclusion by welcoming and aiding international students, students of color, and many more students coming from all sorts of backgrounds.

Honors Computer Science Major, Mathematics & Astronomy Minor

Computer Science is a growing field in the modern era. I believe Computer Science is the key towards increasing the world's efficiency through automation, algorithmic predictions, and more. But why stop at the world? What if we apply our knowledge to the worlds beyond? I hope by combining the mathematics and astronomy minors with Computer Science, the future of our imaginations may become reality.

City Life

I love exploring cities. They illuminate throughout the night and serve as a reminder of human achievement and progress.

Christian

Faith is an important aspect of life. I intend to further grow my faith by attending mass on Sundays, engaging in Bible study, and joining a community.

Vietnamese

Travel
